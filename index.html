    Setup Required

    This app uses OpenWeatherMap (free tier — no credit card needed).
    1. Register at openweathermap.org/api and copy your API key.
    2. Open index.html in a text editor, find YOUR_API_KEY_HERE in the script, and replace it.
    3. Save and reload. New keys activate within 10 min – 2 hours after sign-up.
